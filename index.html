TelFood Apk

Figma Design

Mental Health Tracker Web

Mental Health Tracker (MTH) is a website to help students, especially Telkom University students, maintain their mental health. The website features mood choices that are accumulated every month, articles, and communities to share stories with each other.


Figma Design

SneakShop Apk

SneakShop is an online sneakers sale application. On the app there are various features such as product catalogs, product details, prices, shopping baskets, reservations, and payments. The Sneakshop app will make it easy for buyers to know product details clearly.

Sawit Trace is a website that will be used by Unilever to answer their problems regarding shipping palm oil in Indonesia. Sawit Trace is a project between Telkom University lecturers and Unilever which is used as a proposal if there is an agreement between the two parties.

Daily Mood Tracker is an application that allows users to know the mood tracker that can be searched every day. On that app, users can know the history of each day of the mood that has been selected in the monthly range. Featured features like articles, chatbots, and notes.
